## Knowledge as a Sacred Stewardship

We honor the CommonGround rejection of intelligence built from supremacy, extraction, or indiscriminate aggregation.

Instead, we center knowledge that is:

- **Intentionally Curated**  
  Gathered with a specific sense of purpose and authentic care.

- **Collectively Validated**  
  Honored and defined by the community it serves.

- **Open to Inspection**  
  Transparent, accessible, and legible to all who inhabit the field and all who live on Earth as a collective whole.

- **Evolving Accountability**  
  Rooted in the well-being of those it affects, particularly regarding duty of care and moral injury.

This shifts our work and center of gravity from processing data to tending and nurturing a collective commons.
